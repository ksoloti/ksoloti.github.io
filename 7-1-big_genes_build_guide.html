WIP you for choosing Ksoloti - A Sound Business for Sound Businesses.

Now go ahead and patch some objects or whatever. The ksoloti-objects library included in the Ksoloti Patcher has a set of dedicated objects for Gills under "ksoloti/gills". "pot p" for example will read one of the ten pots and provide you with a unipolar value between 0 and 64. "pot b" will do the same but will give you a bipolar value between -64 and +64. Most inlets expect a certain range and polarity - you can see little + or ± symbols next to some. It is convenient to have dedicated objects for the right polarity as it saves you from having to add a conversion object in the signal chain. Less clutter, more fun.
